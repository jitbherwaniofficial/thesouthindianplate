What is Idli?

Idli is a traditional South Indian steamed rice cake, made from fermented rice and lentil batter. Soft, fluffy, and nutritious, it's a popular breakfast dish enjoyed with chutney or sambhar.

How do you prepare Idli?

We prepare our idlis in homelike environment by fermenting a blend of rice and urad dal (black gram) overnight. Then we pour the batter into idli molds and steamed for 10-15 minutes, resulting in soft, fluffy idlis. Then we serve fresh and hot Idlis with delicious chutney or sambhar.
