Dal Vada : A Crispy South Indian Snack

Dalvada is a traditional Chana Dal Snack. It is also known as Chana Dal Vada. The crispiness and flavory dalvada is a go to snack for most people. It is made from a batter of chana dal, which is soaked, ground, and seasoned with spices like cumin, black pepper, and curry leaves. This batter is then carved into flat shape and then deep-fried to get a golden and crispy exterior. Dalvada is often enjoyed as a breakfast item or snack and typically served with Green chutney.

What is Dal Vada?
